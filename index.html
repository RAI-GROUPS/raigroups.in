Automatic Power Factor Correction (APFC) Panels

Designed to maintain target power factor (PF) on electrical grids, eliminating reactive surcharge penalties. Features microprocessor-controlled controllers.

Parameter Specification
Controller Steps 6, 8, 12, or 16 Stage Control
Capacitor Type Heavy Duty MPP / Gas Filled (APP on request)
Target Power Factor 0.99 (Programmable Lag/Lead)
Switching Mechanism Thyristor Switched / Capacitor Duty Contactors
Harmonic Mitigation Detuned Reactors (7% / 14%) integrated
